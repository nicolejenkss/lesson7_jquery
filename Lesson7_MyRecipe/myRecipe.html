Sugar Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flower
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375.

In a small bowl, stir together flour, baking soda, and baking powder. Set Aside.

In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until smooth.

Beat in egg and vailla.

Gradually blend in dry ingredients.

Roll rounded teaspoons of dough into balls, and place onto ungreased cookie sheets.

Bake 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven.

Let stand on cookie sheet two minutes before removing to cool on wire racks.
